03 — Experimental film

Bloodline — Umbilical cord — Expectation

The film frames expectation as an inherited structure: a downward transmission that is materially intimate and therefore difficult to refuse. Threads visualize how pressure accumulates across generations—where “care” and “control” become indistinguishable at the scale of the body.

By staging kinship as an apparatus, the work asks how family authority is reproduced not primarily through violence, but through ordinary repetition—small instructions that sediment into fate.
