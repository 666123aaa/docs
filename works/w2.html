STATEMENT
A collaborative interactive prototype extending my video work. The satirical maze examines how agency is generated within gendered power structures. A biologically female predatory-animal avatar (presented without stylisation or gender markers) is guided toward an exit composed of cosmetic/body-shaping objects—false lashes, slimming stockings, diet pills— signalling a platform-coded feminine script. Timed pop-ups interrupt progression, modelling hesitation under structural pressure. Multiple endings expose how "choice" is continually shaped, prompting reflection on where subjectivity is positioned within this system.
