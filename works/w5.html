05 — Interactive installation

The Unbearable Weight of Being (Inherited Threads)

The installation stages inheritance as an affective technology: threads and notes map how expectations are transmitted across generations, binding the body through intimacy rather than coercion. Participation becomes a method—visitors externalize, relocate, and reassign expectations to expose how "family" organizes obligation.
