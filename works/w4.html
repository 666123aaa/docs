04 — Sound work

Be quieter

Time: 2024.3 – 2024.4
Keywords: First-person soundscape · Chinese family expectations · Psychological impact

This first-person soundscape examines the psychological effects of premature and overly heavy parental expectations in Chinese families. Expectation-laden phrases surround the listener, then distort, compress, and refract as pressure accumulates.

Ambient environments move across life stages—from embryo, childhood, and adolescence to adulthood and death—showing how expectations begin before agency is possible. The narrative then rewinds back toward the beginning until silence: an "invisible hands" gesture pressing play and rewind on a lifetime.
